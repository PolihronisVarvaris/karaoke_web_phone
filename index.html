How to Use Karaoke Connect

  1. Copy the Room ID or scan the QR code with your phone
  2. Allow microphone access on your phone when prompted
  3. Paste a YouTube URL and click "Extract Lyrics" to get synchronized lyrics
  4. Click "Start Karaoke Session" to begin your performance
  5. Sing along with the highlighted lyrics using your phone as a microphone!
